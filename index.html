An Online Fuzzy Fraud Detection Framework For Credit Card Transactions

Tools: Python, Fuzzy Logistic Regression, Monte Carlo Simulations, Probabilistic Sampling. Dataset: Real Transaction Dataset (European Transactions), 284,807 transactions (492 fraudulent), 29 predictors (V1-V28 are masked due to privacy). Methodology: The study uses Fuzzy Logistic Regression to detect fraudulent credit card transactions in real-time, addressing class imbalance, non-stationary fraud patterns, and separation issues. It follows a three-stage process (Tuning ‚Üí Learning ‚Üí Online Implementation) to optimize fuzzy coefficients, classify transactions using fuzzy thresholds, and efficiently detect fraud with high accuracy.
